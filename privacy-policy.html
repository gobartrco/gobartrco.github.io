Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE DATE: JULY 23, 2025

DATE OF LATEST UPDATE: JULY 24, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Bartr LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards personal information when you use our platform and services (“Service”), including the mobile app and website. It forms part of our Terms of Service.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide Directly

  • Account & Profile Data: Full name, email address, phone number, shipping/billing address, date of birth.
  • Listings & Transactions: Item descriptions, photographs, declared value, trade terms.
  • Communication Data: Messages exchanged with other users, support inquiries, dispute submissions.

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage & Device Data: IP address, browser or app version, device identifiers, operating system, and usage logs.
  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies: Cookies, pixels, local storage, analytics tools like Google Analytics for service performance and usage insights.

c. Information from Third Parties

  • Payment Data: Securely processed by third-party processors (e.g., names, transaction records—not full card numbers).
  • Publicly Available or Affiliate Data: Reputation score data or verification documents shared from partner services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage user accounts, facilitate trades, process holds/refunds, and verify identities.
  • To operate, analyze, and improve our Service—e.g., performance monitoring, fraud detection, user behavior analysis.
  • To communicate updates, marketing, or policy changes (opt-in where required).
  • To support dispute resolution and claims processes, including evidence review and reimbursements.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect against illegal or unauthorized activities.

3. How We Share Your Information

a. Service Providers & Partners

We may share necessary personal information with third parties who support Service operations, including:

  • Payment processors
  • Shipping insurers and carriers.
  • Cloud hosting, analytics, and customer support platforms.

Each third party is contractually bound to handle your data securely and only for designated purposes.

b. Legal & Safety Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law enforcement, for legal compliance, or in good-faith belief it is necessary to:

  • Protect rights, property, or safety.
  • Prevent fraud or security breaches.
  • Comply with subpoenas, litigation, or regulations.

c. Business Transfers

If we undergo a merger, sale, or asset transfer, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction—only under confidentiality obligations and consistent with this Privacy Policy.

d. With Your Consent

With your explicit permission, we may share data for other purposes you agree to at the moment of sharing.

4. Your Choices & Rights

  • Access, Correction & Deletion: You may request to review, update, or delete your personal information via email or in-app tools.
  • Cookie & Tracking Control: Browser or device settings may allow you to block or delete cookies; this may affect Service functionality.
  • Marketing Opt-Out: You can unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time. Transactional emails (e.g., trade confirmation) cannot be opted-out.
  • California & GDPR Rights:
    • California Residents: Rights under CCPA including access, deletion, and non-discrimination. We do not sell personal information.
    • EU / UK Residents (GDPR): Rights to access, rectification, erasure, objection, and portability. Upon request, we will comply unless we have a lawful reason not to.

5. Data Retention & Security

  • We retain personal information as long as needed to fulfill the purposes outlined (e.g., transaction history, dispute windows, legal compliance).
  • We may retain anonymized or aggregated data indefinitely.
  • We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect data (e.g. encryption, secure servers, access control).

6. Children’s Policy

Our Service is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe your child has provided us data without parental consent, please contact us for removal.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. If we make material changes, we will post the revised policy with an updated effective date and—if required—provide notice via email or in-app notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
