Trade Smarter

Bartr's Trade Protection is designed to remove the biggest risk in peer-to-peer trades: trust. Instead of hoping the other party ships as promised, Bartr provides a structured, secure process that protects both sides of the exchange.

What Is Trade Protection?

Trade Protection is Bartr's built-in safety system that ensures every protected trade is completed fairly.

Bartr acts as a neutral safeguard between traders. Both parties commit to the deal, ship their items, and Bartr verifies that everything matches the agreed terms before the trade is finalized.

No guesswork. No blind trust. No crossed fingers.

Why It Matters

Online trades can go wrong in ways that are frustrating and expensive:

  • Items never ship
  • Gear arrives damaged or misrepresented
  • One party backs out mid-trade
  • Disputes with no clear resolution

Trade Protection exists to prevent these situations and give traders confidence to move forward.
